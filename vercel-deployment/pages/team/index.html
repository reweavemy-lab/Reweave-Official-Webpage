Leila Nguyen

Marco Rossi

Pattern Maker

Zara Johnson

Raj Mehta

Production Manager

Elena Santos

Materials Sourcing Specialist

Elena researches and sources sustainable materials for our collections, from organic cotton to innovative recycled fabrics, ensuring they meet our environmental and ethical standards.

Thomas Lee

Artisan Partnership Coordinator

Thomas works directly with our artisan partners around the world, facilitating communication, providing support, and ensuring fair compensation and working conditions.

Amara Okafor

Sustainability Manager

Miguel Fernandez

Miguel measures and reports on the social impact of our work, from job creation to cultural preservation, and develops programs to enhance our positive influence in artisan communities.

Sarah Kim

James Wilson

Marketing Director

James leads our marketing strategy, telling Reweave's story in authentic ways that connect with conscious consumers and highlight the value of sustainable fashion.

Priya Sharma

Leo Chen

Visual Storyteller

Olivia Park

Technology Director

Mateo Rivera
