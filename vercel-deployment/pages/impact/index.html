Our 2025 Sustainability Goals

100% Sustainable Materials

Transition our entire product line to use only certified organic, recycled, or regeneratively sourced materials.

Carbon Neutral Operations

Achieve carbon neutrality across our entire supply chain through renewable energy and carbon offset programs.

1,000 Artisan Livelihoods

Expand our artisan network to support 1,000 skilled craftspeople with fair wages and sustainable livelihoods.

Zero Waste Packaging

Eliminate all plastic from our packaging and ensure all materials are compostable or infinitely recyclable.

Water Stewardship

Reduce water usage by 50% and implement closed-loop water systems in all production facilities.

Regenerative Impact

Move beyond sustainability to create regenerative systems that restore ecosystems and communities.
