Our Story

Reweaving the fabric of fashion through tradition, sustainability, and community.

How It All Began

Reweave was born from a simple yet profound realization during a journey through the textile villages of India. Our founder, Maya Chen, witnessed firsthand the incredible artistry of traditional weavers and the challenges they faced in a rapidly changing global market.

What began as a small project to connect these artisans with conscious consumers has grown into a movement that celebrates cultural heritage, champions sustainable practices, and creates meaningful livelihoods for communities around the world.

Today, Reweave partners with over 200 artisans across 12 countries, bringing their exceptional craftsmanship to a global audience while ensuring fair compensation and sustainable production methods.

Reweave launches with a small collection of handwoven scarves made by artisans in Kutch, India.
