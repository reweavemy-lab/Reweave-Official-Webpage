How It All Began

Reweave began with a simple question. What if everyday goods could carry culture, craft, and impact at the same time?

Started by Howie Sim, Amyla Atiqah, Yeo Jia Yuan, Viman Vinesh, and Adz Arieff during McKinsey Youth Leadership Academy, the team explored how surplus materials and local craftsmanship could come together. What started as a 12 week challenge grew into a mission to uplift B40 single mothers through fair work and modern product design.

After YLA, Reweave was formally incorporated by Howie Sim, Amyla Atiqah, and Jeany Tan, who was the YLA ambassador that guided the team. Together they built Reweave into a lifestyle brand shaped by culture, community, and conscious design.
